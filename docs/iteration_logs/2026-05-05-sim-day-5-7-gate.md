combat. The fleet-count, ship-count, and ownership-flip mismatches all
trace to the same upstream cause.

## Notes for future-me

- **Real Phase 4 was NOT a stub modification — it was a full rewrite.**
  The stub had a target_planet_id-based ETA shortcut that was never how
  env works. Real Phase 4 ignores target_planet_id entirely; collisions
  are checked against ALL planets on the segment.
- **Phase 2 spawn-position bug had been latent in Day 3-5.** Fleets
  spawned at planet center would self-collide under real Phase 4. The
  Day 3-5 filter (no fleets in flight) and gate categories (excluding
  fleet-related) both happened to mask it. Fixed in `b44a094`.
- **Day 3-5 regression check after Phase 4 land** showed match rate
  unchanged (100%) AND mismatch_categories went from `{fleet-position-drift:
  190, fleet-count-mismatch: 4, fleet-id-set-mismatch: 4}` to `{}` —
  real Phase 4 even cleaned up the spawned-this-turn fleets that the
  Day 3-5 stub couldn't handle. (The 1012 vs 1321 triple count
  difference is just env RNG variance across runs, not a methodological
  change.)
- **Phase 5 is the next clear lever** to push the gate from 96% to 99%+.
  No other phase currently in the gate is contributing meaningful error.
